Our Heritage

The Mvule Story

Named after Africa's most resilient tree.

The Mvule — Milicia excelsa — is one of East Africa's hardest, most durable hardwoods. Found across Uganda, Kenya, and Tanzania, it grows slowly, withstands the harshest conditions, and lasts for generations.

That is the standard we hold our technology to. Built for longevity. Engineered for resilience. Grown from the ground up in Africa.
